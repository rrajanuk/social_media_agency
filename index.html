Frequently Asked Questions

How much of my time is really required?

Maximum 45 minutes per week total, spread however works best for you. Quick calls, voice notes, text messages — whatever is easiest. That's it.

Do you guarantee a specific number of leads?

We guarantee consistency and professional execution. Qualified inbound leads are the natural outcome for most clients within 90–180 days, but we can't control exactly when prospects reach out. What we can promise: your profiles will be active, professional, and working for you every single day.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel or pause with 30 days' notice. No long-term contracts.

What if I don't have ideas to share?

That's normal! We guide you with questions and prompts. Most clients find the 45 minutes is easier than expected once we're in a rhythm. We do the hard work of turning your quick thoughts into polished posts.

Which platforms do you recommend?

For financial advisors and consultants, we typically recommend LinkedIn as the foundation, then add Instagram or X. We can manage any platform, but these three tend to deliver the best ROI for professional services.
